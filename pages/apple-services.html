The Problem

Apple devices in business environments are often unmanaged — and that's a real risk.

Many Texas Hill Country businesses run Macs and iPhones alongside Windows systems with no consistent management, no MDM enrollment, and no visibility into what's actually happening on those devices. That creates security gaps, support headaches, and compliance exposure.

Consumer Apple accounts, no remote wipe capability, and staff sharing credentials are far more common than they should be. CAER Technologies fixes this for good.

The Hidden Risk in Unmanaged Apple Devices

A departing employee keeps access to company data on their personal iCloud. A lost iPhone has no remote wipe enabled. A Mac hasn't received a security patch in eight months. These scenarios are common — and preventable.

"If you can't see it, you can't secure it."
