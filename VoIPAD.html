Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions About Business VoIP

Can I keep my current phone number?

Yes. We can port your existing numbers to the new system in most cases.

How long does setup take?

Most systems are up and running within a few days, depending on the size of your business and number transfer timing.

Do I need new phones?

Not always. We can often use compatible existing hardware or provide new desk phones if needed.

What happens if the internet goes down?

Calls can often be routed to mobile devices or backup numbers so you do not lose contact with customers.

Can staff use their cell phones for business calls?

Yes. Your team can make and receive business calls from mobile devices while keeping their personal numbers private.

Is VoIP reliable for business use?

Yes. When properly configured, VoIP is flexible, reliable, and a strong fit for modern businesses.
