Which Plan Is Right for You?

Not sure where you fit? Most businesses start with Sentinel or Guardian, then move into Command as they want fuller support and clearer IT ownership.

Choose Sentinel

If you want basic protection, monitoring, and a trusted IT contact for a very small office.

Choose Guardian

If you want proactive support for a small office and need someone more involved in day-to-day IT.

Choose Command

If you want a real managed IT partner handling the ongoing care of your environment.

Choose Fortress or Sovereign

If your security, compliance, or business risk profile requires stronger protection and oversight.
